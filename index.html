SNU Open 2018

SNUbik's, the cubing club of SNU within the Mathematics Society of SNU, is proud to bring to you the second edition of SNU Open, which is to be held on April 7th and 8th, 2018.

This event is bigger than ever before, with all 18 WCA events being held over the span of the two days, with exciting prizes awaiting the winners. Held in the sprawling 286-acre campus of Shiv Nadar University, we invite you to SNU Open 2018 -- the biggest and the best yet.

REGISTRATION

Fee Structure

A base fees of INR 150 and additional fees of INR 50 per event is applicable till 14 February after which it will increase as shown below. For example, if you do 3 events, your fees is 150 + (3 x 50 )= INR 300.

Date Base Fee Event Fee Free for SNU Students
Upto 14 February 150 50 Yes
15 Feb - 31 March 250 50 Yes
On the Spot Registration 350 50 No

Note : Registration is free for SNU Students. Not Available for On the Spot Registration.

Steps to Register

  1. Register on the WCA website. (LINK)
  2. Pay the registration fee through one of the below options:
    1. Calculate your payment amount and transfer through Paytm to 8447852838. In the description, mention SNU 2018, your name and WCA ID(if any).
    2. Pay through Instamojo (LINK).
  3. SNU students need to register at this link in additional of WCA website instead of making a payment. (LINK)
  4. Wait upto 48 hours for your name to appear in list of registered participants. (LINK)
  5. Your name will continue to appear in the waiting list if you do not make a payment but register on the WCA website. Your name being in waiting list does not mean you need to wait to pay.
  6. Online Registration closes on 31st March, 2018, 23:59 IST.

On the Spot Registration

  1. This will be available from 9:00 to 10:00 A.M. on 7th April.
  2. A base fees of INR 350 and additional fees of INR 50 per event is applicable. (i.e. INR 100 more than online fees)
  3. This is mainly for those who forget to register in due time. If you are facing problems with online payment, please write to us and we will be happy to assist you.
  4. Pre-registration on WCA website by 31st March is Mandatory.
