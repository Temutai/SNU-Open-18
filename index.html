SNU Open 2017

Hola, cubers! Love puzzling your minds by solving Rubik's cubes and other twisty puzzles? Well, great! We got a two-day competition just for you. Rubik's cubes, Professor's cubes, Rubik's Revenge, Pyraminxes and much more! 12 events spread over a span of two days with the most exciting of prizes waiting for the winners. The need for a laser-sharp focus, the unsettling nervousness caused by an ever watchful audience, sweaty hands and a craving to go against the clock. Fun, yes? We thought so. Held in the 286 acre campus of Shiv Nadar University, we invite you to our college's first World Cubing Association recognised cubing event. If you are a cuber, then what are you waiting for? GO! Register, close this window, shut down your PC and practise! Because the best is yet to come.
